Privacy Notice

Last updated: April 28, 2026

This is a simple notice about how your data is handled when you subscribe to the rkrk.io newsletter. I believe in transparency — no fine print tricks.

What I collect

  • Email address — so I can send you the newsletter.
  • Consent status — a record that you agreed to receive emails.
  • Timestamp — when you subscribed.

How I use it

Only to send you the rkrk.io newsletter — language learning insights, teaching philosophy updates, and related content from Kee. Nothing else. No selling. No sharing.

Where it's stored

Securely on Supabase (a GDPR-compliant cloud database service based in the US). Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

Your rights

  • Unsubscribe anytime — every email has an unsubscribe link.
  • Request deletion — reply to any email asking me to delete your data.
  • Access your data — ask me what I have, and I'll tell you.

Cookies

This site does not use tracking cookies or analytics. The only "cookie-like" thing is Supabase's session handling if you log into any future account features (which is opt-in).

Data security

The website is served over HTTPS (Vercel). Your data is stored in Supabase with row-level security — only I can access the subscriber list. The subscription form uses Supabase's anonymous key with strict insert-only permissions, so no one can read or modify data through the website.

Contact

Questions about your data? Reply to any newsletter email, or reach out via the contact info on rkrk.io.

This notice is a good-faith effort at transparency. I'm a solo educator running a small site, not a legal department. If you have specific compliance concerns, let's talk.
