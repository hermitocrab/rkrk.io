Part 3 — IELTS Deep Dive: Cue Card Breakdown

15 minutes · AI-Assisted Lesson Planning
📋 IELTS Speaking Part 2 Cue Card
Describe a place you visited that was affected by pollution.
  • Where this place is
  • When you visited it
  • What kind of pollution you saw there
  • And explain how this experience made you feel

📝 Model Answer Structure

Paragraph 1 · Introduction
Name the place, set the scene. "I'd like to talk about a river in my hometown that I visited a few years ago. It's called the Qinghe River and it runs through the eastern part of the city."
Paragraph 2 · The Pollution
Describe what you saw, heard, smelled. Use sensory vocabulary. "The most striking thing was the colour of the water — it was a murky brown, with patches of foam floating on the surface. The air smelled sharp and chemical."
Paragraph 3 · Cause & Effect
Explain the source of pollution. "I later learned that factories upstream had been discharging industrial waste into the river for years. Local residents told me that cases of respiratory problems had increased noticeably."
Paragraph 4 · Personal Reflection
How it made you feel + what happened next. "It was genuinely upsetting. But there is a positive side — the local government has since launched a cleanup project, and the water quality is slowly improving."
