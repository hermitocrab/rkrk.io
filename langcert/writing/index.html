Section 2 · 20 min

📊 Part 1 · 图表报告四段式

Introduction → Trend Description → Reason Explanation → Prediction & Evaluation

🏗️ The 4-Part Framework

❶ Introduction 开头段

简洁说明图表内容与观察时间。不加入数据或个人观点。使用 paraphrase 技巧改写题干。

❷ Trend Description 趋势描述

识别高低点、上升下降、交叉点、反转点。使用趋势动词短语 + 具体数据。

❸ Reason Explanation 原因分析

结合题干背景信息，用因果连接词分析变化原因。引用政策、市场、社会等因素。

❹ Prediction + Evaluation 预测评价

根据已有趋势评论预测是否合理，展现批判性思维。可以说「optimistic」「realistic」「unlikely」。

✏️ ❶ Introduction 模板句

Sentence Patterns

"The graph/chart/table illustrates/shows the changes in ___ over a period of ___ years."

"It provides information about ___ between Year X and Year Y in [location]."

"This report outlines the trends in ___ in the past decade, based on the given data."

🔮 ❹ Prediction & Evaluation

预测句

"If this trend continues, it is likely that ___ will reach ___ by Year ___."

"It is predicted that... / expected to... / likely to..."

评价句（批判性思维）

"However, the forecast appears overly optimistic given the current slowdown."

"In my opinion, the prediction is realistic, especially if ___ continues to ___."

"Nevertheless, external factors such as ___ might affect the accuracy of this forecast."

📝 真题拆解 · Milton-on-Sea 旅游收入

Real Task 1 with annotated Band 7+ model answer from the official PDF

🖼️ Task Prompt · 真题题干

4 个行业 · 10 年数据 · 新主题公园 + 高铁 + Airbnb 多因素交叉

⭐ Band 7+ Model Answer · 逐段标注

Introduction 开头
As can be seen from the graph, income from international tourism fell in all four sectors from Year 1 to Year 8. Resort hotels in Milton-on-Sea used to receive the largest proportion of the money, but income fell from around £800,000 in Year 1 to around 350,000 in Year 8. Attractions and food outlets experienced the same trend, as did hire cars and taxis.
Trend + Reason 趋势+原因
However, in Year 9, three of the four sectors saw a large rise in income. Presumably this was as a result of the opening of a new theme park and the new high-speed train link with the capital in Year 8. Although in Years 9 and 10, income for resort hotels continued to decline, all other tourist income sectors rose. Presumably many of the tourists coming to Milton-on-Sea because of improved rail transport stayed in private rental rather than resort hotels.
Prediction + Evaluation 预测评价
If the trend continues as the tourist board predicts, total income from all four sectors might increase to well over £1 million by the end of the next two years. And if restrictions on the number of Airbnb rooms are put in place, then the resort hotel's income could well increase too.

🔑 Key Phrases to Steal

experienced the same trend, as did...

Section 4 · 25 min

✍️ Part 1 实战 · Fizzy Drinks vs Juice

Guided Practice -- sentence frames → self-check → model answer

🖼️ Task Prompt · 真题

🧩 Sentence Frames · 句子骨架

用以下框架完成你的报告，填空后组合成完整段落----考察：趋势识别 + 政策因果 + 批判性预测

Introduction

As can be seen from the graph, sales of fizzy drinks had __________ while juice sales had __________ before the change in the law at the end of Year 2.

Reason Analysis

It seems that as a result of __________, sales of fizzy drinks __________ significantly in Year 3. Sales of juice, meanwhile, more than __________.

Trend + Policy Response

Both of these trends continued in Year 4 but it is clear that __________. At the end of Year 4, a law was passed __________. This seems to have had the desired effect because, in Year 5, __________.

Prediction + Critical Evaluation

If the trends in the sales of both types of cold drink continues, in the next 5 years, __________. However, it is likely that the changes will __________ as price is not the only factor in determining cold drink choice.

📝 Band 7+ Model Answer (from official PDF)

As can be seen from the graph, sales of fizzy drinks had risen while juice sales had fallen steeply before the change in the law at the end of Year 2. It seems that as a result of the reduction in the price of juice, sales of fizzy drinks fell significantly in Year 3. Sales of juice, meanwhile, more than doubled.

Both of these trends continued in Year 4 but it is clear that the government did not feel the movement was fast enough. At the end of Year 4, a law was passed increasing the price of fizzy drinks. This seems to have had the desired effect because, in Year 5, sales of juice nearly reached the same level as fizzy drinks.

If the trends in the sales of both types of cold drink continues, in the next 5 years, sales of juice will far exceed that of fizzy drinks. However, it is likely that the changes will flatten out as price is not the only factor in determining cold drink choice.

✅ Self-Check · 自检清单

  • ✓ 覆盖了题干所有要点：趋势 + 原因 + 预测
  • ✓ 使用了至少 3 个趋势动词（rise / fall / double / exceed）
  • ✓ 使用了因果连接词（as a result of / because / seems to have had）
  • ✓ 有预测段 + 批判性评价（flatten out / not the only factor）
  • ✓ 词数控制在 150-200 之间

📝 Part 2 · 议论文四段式 + 真题题库

Essay Structure -- Introduction → Viewpoint 1 → Viewpoint 2 → Personal Opinion

🏗️ Essay 4-Part Framework

❶ Introduction 引出话题

引出话题，简要展示两方观点/问题背景。Paraphrase 题干，不急于表态。

❷ Viewpoint 1 正方论点

主题句 + 原因解释 + 例子说明。客观呈现支持方的论据。

❸ Viewpoint 2 反方论点

同结构，注意逻辑对比。呈现反对方的顾虑与论据。

❹ Personal Opinion 个人立场

明确表态 + 总结理由。可以部分支持/反对，平衡观点更高分。

⚖️ Part 1 vs Part 2 · 关键对比

✏️ Template Sentences by Section

❶ Introduction

"There has been considerable debate over whether..." / "Some people argue that..., while others believe that..."

❷ Viewpoint 1 (Support)

"Supporters of this idea claim that... One key advantage is that... For example, studies have shown that..."

❸ Viewpoint 2 (Oppose)

"On the other hand, critics argue that... A major concern is that... They believe this might lead to..."

❹ Personal Opinion + Conclusion

"In my opinion, while both sides have valid points, I believe that... To conclude, a balanced approach may be the most effective."

⭐ 范文拆解 · AI 在教育中的使用

📝 Band 7+ Model Answer (from official PDF) ~250 words

Introduction 引出话题
Artificial intelligence (AI) in schools and higher education is being talked about a lot. Some people say it's good because it could help students find information for their studies more quickly. Others disagree, believing that it might make students lazy and stop them from thinking on their own.
Viewpoint 1 支持方
The people who like AI in education say it is like a clever helper. They think that, because it is a learning system, it will gradually adjust to the level of each user. It will learn to provide information in the best way to suit the learner.
Viewpoint 2 反对方
However, there are people who worry about using too much AI in education. They think if students always use AI, they might forget how to find information themselves and, perhaps even more importantly, how to come up their own ideas. They think AI could stop students from being creative.
Personal Opinion 个人立场
In my opinion, it's good to use AI in all levels of education, but not too overuse it. AI can help students learn and find things, but teachers need to teach students how to think and learn on their own in addition. It's important, therefore, to learn how to check if facts are right and whether it is the latest information about a topic.
