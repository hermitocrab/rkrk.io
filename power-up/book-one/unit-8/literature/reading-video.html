🧠 故事导图 · Story Mind Map

👤 Characters 角色

Rob is at Sue's home today.

Sue is kind and plays with Rob.

📍 Setting 场景

The story is set at Sue's home.

They play in the bedroom, garden, kitchen, and hall.

📖 Story 故事情节

Rob asks Sue to play with an ugly old ball.

They throw, catch, bounce, and kick the ball.

The ball hits the clock on the wall.

The clock comes down and hits the mat.

Rob says sorry for that.

Sue picks up the clock and says it's OK.

💡 Lesson 启示

It is not a good idea to play ball in the house.

A good friend says it's OK and doesn't worry.
