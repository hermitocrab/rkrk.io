📜 PU1 Unit 8 · Literature

Poem: Play Ball · 诗歌：一起玩球

🎭 This Week's Story

Rob visits Sue and wants to play ball. They throw, catch, bounce, and kick the ball all around the house — but then the ball hits the clock! Sue picks it up and says it's OK. A poem about play, accidents, and friendship.
