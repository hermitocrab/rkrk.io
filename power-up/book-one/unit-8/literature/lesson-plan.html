Part 1 — Warm Up & Vocabulary (~55min)

1

🎯 Warm Up — Places Quiz

Show slides asking "Which place is good for this activity?" (park, woods, swimming pool, etc.) Students answer and explain why. Activate background knowledge about different places.

⏱ 10 min
2

📖 Vocabulary Introduction — 12 Words

Introduce each vocabulary word with PPT: mat, reply, pick up, point, worry, bounce, throw, catch, kick, poem, title, garden. Students repeat each word 3 times. Use TPR for action verbs (bounce, throw, catch, kick).

⏱ 15 min
3

🎮 Vocabulary Practice — Action Games

TPR game: teacher says "bounce" → students mime bouncing. "throw" → mime throwing. Speed round: quick recognition. Each student takes turns being the caller.

⏱ 10 min
4

📖 First Reading — Poem Introduction

Teacher reads the poem aloud with expression. Students listen and follow along. Discuss: What is a poem? What is a title? Who are Rob and Sue?

⏱ 10 min
5

🗣️ Shared Reading + Questions

Read poem together. Pause after each stanza to ask comprehension questions from PPT. Each student answers individually. Practice "reply" and "point" in context.

⏱ 10 min

💪🕺 Just Dance Break!

Stand up, move, dance! Refresh for Part 2.

Part 2 — Story Map & Retell (~55min)

6

🧠 Story Map — Characters & Setting

Build the story map together. Who are the characters? (Rob and Sue) Where are they? (Sue's home) What room do they play in? (bedroom, garden, kitchen, hall). Students add to the map.

⏱ 12 min
7

📖 Story Map — Plot Sequence

Map out the story events: Rob asks to play → They throw/catch/bounce/kick → Ball hits clock → Clock falls on mat → Rob says sorry → Sue says it's OK. Use fill-in-blank slides.

⏱ 12 min
8

💡 Story Map — Lesson & Retell Practice

Discuss: What lesson do we learn? (Don't play ball in the house / Friends forgive). Each student retells the full story using the story map. Teacher gives individual feedback.

⏱ 12 min
9

🎬 Guided Reading Video + Fill-in-Blank

Watch the mindmap video together. Students complete fill-in-blank poem slides from PPT independently. Check answers as a group.

⏱ 10 min
10

✅ Wrap Up — Review & Homework

Quick review: each student names 3 new words they learned. Assign homework: read poem 3×, practice vocabulary, retell story.

⏱ 9 min
