The Problem

The Gap Between Learning and Living

200 million Chinese learners study English. Most pass exams but cannot operate independently in an English-speaking environment. They can score IELTS 6.5 but cannot register with a GP, write a university application email, or participate in a seminar discussion.

Agencies fill the gap — but they handle logistics, not capability. They process documents. They don't build learners. They sell placement. Not preparation.
