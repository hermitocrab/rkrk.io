The Journey

At Bangor University, Matt became the only person in the world to graduate in Marine Botany in 1986. A PhD followed — but when his supervisor fell ill, he built his own destiny, collaborating across Britain's top institutions on the landmark Lynne Brianne Project.

When research funding dried up, he pivoted hard. 1987: a failing hospitality unit → five-figure profit in two years. 1991: first bar. Twenty years. Thirty-five venues. At Whitbread (Costa Coffee), his team opened a venue that repaid its investment in six months — beating the Dubai flagship.

In 2010, he started again. Teaching qualification earned during lunch breaks. Beijing kindergarten. Chongqing primary school. EF in 2012. Teacher → Senior DoS. Three centres opened. National initiatives. Star Teacher twice, Star Manager once.
