03 · The Moat

RUA: The Pedagogical OS

Anyone can wrap ChatGPT in a quiz maker. Nobody else has RUA — a framework that makes AI actually work for learning, not just chat with a subject filter. It's embedded in every prompt, every app, every interaction.

R

Recognize

Decompose the Target

BEEF Framework: Background, Evidence (CHOP), Explanation (SOOI), Feedback. Break any learning goal into bite-sized, actionable chunks.

U

Understand

Deep Comprehension

3C Method: Collocation, Contexts, Connotation. Go beyond surface definitions to true understanding — what pairs with what, when it applies, what it implies.

A

Apply

Deliberate Practice

GAP System: Gauge the gap, Analyze root causes, Practice with feedback loops. Not just "do more exercises" — targeted drilling with AI-powered diagnostics.

Why RUA Is the Moat

Without RUA
Quiz → "Generate 10 questions about photosynthesis"
AI Tutor → "Answer student questions about math"
Analytics → "Student got 70% correct"
Prompts → Raw text, no pedagogical structure
With RUA
Quiz → Decomposed into R, U, A question types automatically
AI Tutor → Diagnoses which RUA stage student is stuck at
Analytics → "Strong on Recognize, weak on Apply — GAP analysis"
Prompts → BEEF decomposition, 3C context, GAP feedback
