教学理念

From Teacher to Learning Architect 从教师到学习架构师

In the AI era, the teacher who competes with machines on content delivery has already lost. The future belongs to those who design learning experiences — Strategists, Architects, Coaches.

在 AI 时代，试图在内容传递上与机器竞争的教师已经输了。未来属于设计学习体验的人——策略师、架构师、教练。

🔍

R · RecognizeR · 识别

Identify the boundaries of your learning target. Master the art of asking the right questions. Decompose any learning goal into manageable, promptable chunks.

识别学习目标的核心边界。掌握提问的艺术。将任何学习目标分解为可管理的、可提示的模块。

🧠

U · UnderstandU · 理解

Synthesize beyond memorization. Leverage AI to adjust the dial — from ELI5 to PhD level — until the concept clicks in your specific human brain.

超越记忆的综合。利用 AI 拨动理解之轮——从五岁小孩到博士级别——直到概念在你的大脑中豁然开朗。

⚡

A · ApplyA · 应用

Execute in the real world. Find the friction. Feed every failure back into Recognize — turning learning into a cognitive operating system, not a study hack.

在真实世界中执行。找到摩擦点。将每一次失败反馈回识别阶段——把学习变成认知操作系统，而非应试技巧。
